Albuquerque Film Locations

There have been many films, television shows, and commercials shot on location throughout this great city. We constructed this using the ABQ Open Data Portal to visualize which areas are utilized the most by productions. For those of you looking to have a cameo...we suggest you hang out where it's hot!
